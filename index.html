Instructions

Test your memory and stay mentally sharp. It's a classic card flipper game, just match the flipped cards two at a time. Here are the rules specific to this game:

  • You'll receive 20 points per each pair of cards matched
  • You'll receive a point penalty for each pair of cards you mismatch. Each level has it's own point deduction:
    • Easy: 3pt
    • Medium: 2pt
    • Hard: 1pt
  • You'll receive a bonus point multiplier for finding matches earlier on and you'll receive a larger completion bonus the sooner you finish.
